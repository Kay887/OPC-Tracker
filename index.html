Step 3 — Tax & Annual Compliance
Tax
Proprietorship Tax & Filing ObligationsPersonal ITR + GST returns + PT — no corporate tax
0/6 ▶
Understand your tax structure
Proprietorship income is not taxed separately — it is added to your personal income and taxed at individual slab rates. New regime: 0% up to ₹3L, 5% (₹3L–7L), 10% (₹7L–10L), 15% (₹10L–12L), 20% (₹12L–15L), 30% above ₹15L. No ₹12.75L rebate for business income — only salary/other income qualifies for rebate.
File ITR-3 or ITR-4 annually
File ITR-4 (Sugam) if using the presumptive taxation scheme under Section 44ADA — declare 50% of gross receipts as profit (no books required if receipts < ₹50L). File ITR-3 (with books) if actual profits are lower than 50% or if receipts exceed ₹50L.
Deadline: 31 July each year
Section 44ADA — Presumptive Taxation decision
If your professional receipts are under ₹50L/year, Sec 44ADA lets you declare 50% as income without maintaining books of accounts. Massive compliance simplification. Useful when actual expenses are less than 50% of receipts. Consult CA to evaluate annually.
Major compliance simplification
GST Returns (if registered)
Monthly: GSTR-1 (outward supplies) by 11th, GSTR-3B (tax payment) by 20th. Or opt for QRMP scheme (quarterly returns) if turnover < ₹5Cr/year — reduces filings from 24 to 8 per year. Annual return: GSTR-9 by 31 December.
Monthly or quarterly

₹2,500/year if income exceeds ₹10L. Pay at the Karnataka PT portal or through your bank challan. Keep payment receipt — required during GST audit and ITR filing. Due: typically before 31 March each year.

Classify all marks: Word mark vs Logo (Device) mark
File word marks first — they give broader protection (any font, colour, style). File logo/device marks separately only if you have a finalised designed logo. Don't file logo marks with a placeholder design you might change later.
Confirm MSME status (Udyam certificate) for fee discount
Upload Udyam certificate when filing TM-A to claim ₹4,500 rate (vs ₹9,000 at full rate). On 10 marks this saves ₹45,000. Udyam must match the filing entity — ensure Proprietorship Udyam is ready before filing under proprietorship name.
50% cost saving — don't skip
Decide filing entity for each mark
Brand marks (CyberNeurix, Rix umbrella) — file under Proprietorship if that's the commercial entity now. Product marks (CNIS, RixOne) — consider filing under OPC once incorporated. IP ownership should match who will enforce and license the mark. Changing ownership later requires TM-P assignment (₹5,000/mark).
Hard to change later without cost
Search all marks on IP India before filing
Go to ipindiaonline.gov.in → Trademark Search. Search each mark name: CyberNeurix, CNIS, Rix, RixOne, RixLogix, RixTrace, RixControl, RixCloud, RixNexis, RixIntel. Search both phonetically similar names and exact matches. Look in Class 42, 9, and 41 separately.

Ongoing TM Maintenance
Renewal
Trademark Renewal & EnforcementTrademarks last 10 years — renewable indefinitely
0/4 ▶
Set TM renewal calendar reminders (10 years from filing date)
Each mark is valid for 10 years from its filing date, not registration date. Renewal (Form TM-R) must be filed within 6 months before expiry. Late renewal is possible within 1 year after expiry but attracts surcharge. Set calendar reminders 12 months before each mark's expiry.
Use marks continuously to avoid cancellation
A trademark can be cancelled for non-use if you don't use it for 5 consecutive years after registration. Keep evidence of use: website screenshots, invoices with the mark, marketing materials. Maintain a use-evidence folder for each mark.
Non-use for 5 years = cancellable
Watch for infringement and file oppositions if needed
Monitor IP India's TM Journal every 2 weeks for similar marks in Classes 9, 41, 42. If you spot an infringing mark during its 4-month opposition window, file Form TM-O with grounds of opposition (prior use, likelihood of confusion). Missing the window forfeits opposition rights.
Transfer marks to OPC if entity structure changes (TM-P)
If marks were filed under proprietorship but you later want OPC to own them, file Form TM-P (Assignment/Transmission) on IP India. Fee: ₹5,000/mark (govt) + attorney fees. This is a formal process — execute a trademark assignment deed before filing.
₹5,000/mark if assignment needed
