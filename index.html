Proprietorship Costs
A proprietorship has no registration fee, no incorporation process, and no MCA filings. Costs below are the only real expenses — mostly optional or threshold-triggered.
One-time · Threshold triggered
GST Registration
₹0
Self-file on gst.gov.in — no govt fee. Required only if revenue exceeds ₹20L/year or for B2B billing. No CA needed to register.
One-time · Free
Udyam (MSME) Registration
₹0
Free self-registration at udyamregistration.gov.in · Enables MSME trademark fee discount (₹4,500 vs ₹9,000/mark)
Annual · Karnataka only
Professional Tax (PT)
₹2,500/yr
Flat ₹2,500/year if income exceeds ₹10L. Enrolment is free. Self-employed professionals must register with Karnataka PT authority.
Annual · Personal ITR
Income Tax Filing (ITR-3 / ITR-4)
₹0–3,000/yr
Proprietorship income taxed as personal income (individual slabs). Self-file free on incometax.gov.in, or CA charges ₹1,500–₹3,000. No ITR-6, no corporate tax — unlike OPC.
Annual · If GST registered
GST Return Filing
₹0–3,000/yr
Self-file GSTR-1 + GSTR-3B monthly (or quarterly under QRMP). CA charges ₹1,000–₹3,000/year for GST compliance. No separate auditor needed unlike OPC.
One-time · Banking
Current Account Opening
₹0–5,000
Most banks have no fee to open. Some require a minimum balance (₹5,000–₹25,000 depending on bank). No board resolution or CA required — just PAN, Aadhaar, GST cert.
